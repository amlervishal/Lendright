Application Process

Process

Step 1

Share your details of student/co-applicant with representatives of LendRight and share your consent with them to share these details with lenders

Step 2

LendRight will reach out to different lenders and connect them with student/co-applicant

Step 3

After receiving your documents and details, lenders will initiate eligibility process and comeback with respective offerings

Step 4

LendRight will assist students to negotiate and assist in disbursement planning
